# Planner Playbook (Draft Outline)

This document will serve as a practical guide for planners using the forecasting and inventory dashboard.

---

## Weekly planning workflow
- Review forecast accuracy and bias
- Identify SKUs with elevated stockout or overstock risk
- Adjust inventory policies for exception items
- Document decisions and assumptions

---

## Key dashboard signals
- Forecast error trends
- Bias indicators
- Service-level risk alerts
- Inventory exposure by segment

---

## Decision guidelines
- When to increase safety stock
- When to accept lower service levels
- How to respond to demand or lead-time shocks
